02 / THE CONSTRAINT

Credibility was there. The channel wasn't built.

  • Founder profiles weren't optimized for conversion
  • Company content was too product-focused or inconsistent
  • Outbound was manual, inconsistent, or disconnected from content
  • The market needed warming before any outreach
  • No system connected content, targeting, messaging, replies, and calls
The brief

Build a repeatable LinkedIn growth system combining founder-led content, company positioning, targeted prospecting, LinkedIn outbound, email follow-up, and weekly KPI tracking.

03 / SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

What we built

Five connected blocks. Each one feeds the next — content warms the market, outbound starts the conversation, tracking sharpens both.

01

Founder positioning

  • Rewrote founder positioning
  • Clarified headline + profile message
  • Complex offers → simple market narratives
  • Profiles built to earn trust before outreach
02

Content engine

  • Founder-led posts
  • Angles: pain points, trends, product narrative, proof
  • Content that warms the market before outbound
  • Educational · opinion · proof · founder POV
3–5 founder posts/wk · 1–2 company posts/wk · weekly review
03

Target account research

  • Segmented target account lists
  • Prioritized by role, company type, relevance, intent
  • Separated by persona and message angle
  • Founders · CEOs · Growth · Marketing · BD · Investors
04

LinkedIn + email outbound

  • LinkedIn connection sequences
  • Short, human first messages
  • Pain-point follow-ups
  • Email outbound support · per-persona messaging
100–150 LinkedIn prospects/wk · 100–200 email/wk · 3–5 touchpoints each
05

KPI tracking & optimization

  • Acceptance · reply · positive-reply rates
  • Calls booked · opportunities created
  • Best angles · best personas
  • Objections + lost reasons logged
Weekly KPI review · weekly message iteration · monthly ICP refinement
